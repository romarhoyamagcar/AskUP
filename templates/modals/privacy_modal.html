Privacy Policy & Data Protection

Your Privacy Matters: This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.
1. Information We Collect
1.1 Personal Information
  • Account Information: Name, email address, username, password
  • Profile Data: Academic interests, grade level, institution (optional)
  • Contact Information: Email for notifications and communications
  • Verification Data: For educator accounts, credentials and qualifications
1.2 Usage Information
  • Activity Data: Questions asked, answers provided, interactions
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information
  • Analytics Data: Page views, session duration, feature usage
  • Communication Data: Messages sent through the platform
1.3 Cookies and Tracking
  • Essential Cookies: Required for platform functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand user behavior
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Third-party Cookies: From integrated services (with consent)
2. How We Use Your Information
2.1 Primary Purposes
  • Service Provision: Enable Q&A functionality and user interactions
  • Account Management: Create, maintain, and secure user accounts
  • Communication: Send notifications, updates, and support responses
  • Quality Assurance: Moderate content and ensure platform safety
2.2 Secondary Purposes
  • Platform Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
  • Research: Anonymized data for educational research (with consent)
  • Marketing: Send relevant educational content and updates (opt-in)
  • Legal Compliance: Meet regulatory requirements and legal obligations
3. Information Sharing and Disclosure
We Never Sell Personal Data: Your personal information is never sold to third parties.
3.1 When We Share Information
  • With Consent: When you explicitly authorize sharing
  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help operate the platform
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or court order
  • Safety Concerns: To protect users from harm or illegal activity
3.2 Public Information
  • Questions and Answers: Visible to all platform users
  • Profile Information: Username and basic profile data
  • Activity Indicators: Participation levels and contributions
  • Anonymous Analytics: Aggregated usage statistics
4. Data Security Measures
4.1 Technical Safeguards
  • Encryption: All data transmitted using SSL/TLS encryption
  • Secure Storage: Data stored on secure, access-controlled servers
  • Authentication: Multi-factor authentication for admin accounts
  • Access Controls: Role-based access to sensitive information
4.2 Operational Safeguards
  • Staff Training: Regular privacy and security training for employees
  • Access Logging: All data access is logged and monitored
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security assessments and updates
  • Incident Response: Procedures for handling security breaches
5. Your Privacy Rights
5.1 Access and Control
  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
5.2 Communication Preferences
  • Email Notifications: Opt out of non-essential emails
  • Marketing Communications: Unsubscribe from promotional content
  • Platform Notifications: Control in-app notification settings
  • Cookie Preferences: Manage cookie settings and consent
6. Data Retention
6.1 Retention Periods
  • Account Data: Retained while account is active plus 2 years
  • Content Data: Questions and answers retained indefinitely for community benefit
  • Usage Data: Analytics data retained for 3 years
  • Communication Data: Support messages retained for 5 years
6.2 Deletion Procedures
  • Account Deletion: Personal identifiers removed, content anonymized
  • Legal Holds: Some data may be retained for legal compliance
  • Backup Systems: Data may persist in backups for up to 90 days
  • Third-party Services: Data deletion from integrated services
7. International Data Transfers
  • Global Service: Data may be processed in multiple countries
  • Adequate Protection: Transfers only to countries with adequate protection
  • Safeguards: Standard contractual clauses for international transfers
  • EU Users: Special protections for European Union residents
8. Children's Privacy
Special Protection: We provide enhanced privacy protection for users under 18.
  • Age Verification: Users must be 13+ to create accounts
  • Parental Consent: Required for users under 18
  • Limited Data Collection: Minimal data collection for minors
  • Enhanced Moderation: Additional content review for young users
9. Third-Party Services
9.1 Integrated Services
  • Analytics: Google Analytics for usage statistics
  • CDN Services: Content delivery networks for performance
  • Email Services: Third-party email delivery providers
  • Payment Processing: Secure payment processors (if applicable)
9.2 External Links
  • Third-party Websites: Not covered by this privacy policy
  • Educational Resources: Links to external learning materials
  • Social Media: Integration with social platforms (optional)
  • User Responsibility: Review privacy policies of linked sites
10. Privacy Policy Updates
  • Regular Reviews: Policy reviewed and updated annually
  • Notification: Users notified of material changes
  • Effective Date: Changes effective 30 days after notification
  • Version Control: Previous versions available upon request
